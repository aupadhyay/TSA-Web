Other Competitions



Verizon Innovative App Challenge

The Verizon App Challenge is a competition in which students are given the chance to turn their ideas into a reality by submitting a short video detailing their mobile app idea. If chosen, they have the chance to work with MIT faculty to make a reality!

TEAMS: Tests of Engineering Aptitude, Mathematics, and Science

TEAMS is a competition that allows our students to participate in something they care about. It creates an environment where students can learn about STEM, and provides them with a spark to boost their passion to the next level.
