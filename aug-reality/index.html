Technology

Most of the augmented reality applications are built for smartphones. These smartphones are perfect of AR as they already have a variety of useful sensors for augmented reality like gyroscopes, GPS units, high quality cameras, and accelerometers. In addition they are easily able to connect to the Internet and contain many interaction mechanisms such as microphones, touch screens, and speakers.

AR is a technology based on powerful image processing(Computer vision Processing). This allows applications to analyze the geometry of the real world and help project information in the right positions. Computer Vision is also used for robot navigation, and automated quality control. Many applications designed for augmented reality require certain components to track movement from the user. Some of these components include gyroscopes, solid-state accelerometers, and MEMS(Microelectromechanical systems). In other cases GPS is used to project important information using location. One example of this is Pokemon GO which makes different pokemon appear based on your location.

Concerns

AR can cause concerns for people in their everyday lives.

Even though it improves our research, commerce, education and recreation, Augmented Reality can be extremely harmful to our safety. One glaring problem is that Augmented Reality sometimes leads to user distraction causing users to be oblivious of the world around them. This can result in car accidents and other disastrous events. Another concern is the security risk based on the newness of this technology. Hackers can easily access your information and will be able to see what you are seeing. Finally, poorly designed AR systems may be able to impair your sight and may cause other harmful effects.
