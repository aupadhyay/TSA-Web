CAREER TECHNICAL EDUCATION

Why are CTE Classes so important? Career and Technical Education courses are classes that helps students with various skills they will need in the future. Students learn how to analyze data, create models, program algorithms, public speaking and much more. These classes allow students to realize their passion or interest early on and help them prepare for their future and college.
